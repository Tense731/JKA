EXPERIENCED TEAM

Established in 1997, Soccer Solutions has over 25 years experience of hosting small sided football games at various venues across Dorset and Hampshire. We pride ourselves as being the friendliest and easiest gateway to play 6-a-side football on the South Coast. Run by Bob Kitley, our competitive but friendly leagues have gained a reputation for being some of the best in Bournemouth, Poole and beyond! We’re active in the community, and we support the Steve Bernard Foundation, a charity which was set up in memory of the affectionately named local footballer “Stevie B”, following his tragic passing in a car accident in November 2005.

EASY TO CONTACT

If you’re unsure of where to begin, you can send us an email
or give us a call, where we will answer as many questions as we
can. Or just visit our Contact Us page and type your message
and we will reply as soon as possible. We make sure our leagues
and cups run smoothly, so we have a friendly on-site co-ordinator
that can answer any questions or deal with any issues you
that you may have. We post results and fixtures on this website as
soon as we can after each set of games, and we’ll try to make sure
as many special requests for kick-off times can be dealt with where possible.

EVERYTHING INCLUDED

Part of the beauty of Soccer Solutions is that we make it
easy for new teams to get involved in our leagues, therefore,
we make sure everything is covered. This includes: Hire of the
astroturf area & floodlighting. full use of individual team changing
facilities and showers, FA Qualified referees, an on site co-ordinator
to ensure smooth running of the league, good quality match balls
and bibs (if required), plus the chance to win quality trophies and
prizes, as well as entry to Sports Centre bar with giant screen Sky
Sports TV. To find out the costs of competing, find out more on our
League Info & Costs page.
